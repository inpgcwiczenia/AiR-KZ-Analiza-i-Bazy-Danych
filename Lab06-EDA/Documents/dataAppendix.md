# Data appendix
All files from /AnalysisData/ are described here.

##  data.csv
This file contains data on vacuum cleaner sales in Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship.
There are too many columns and empty spaces.
\
Variables:
- Dni od zakupu - numerical variable; days since purchase. 
- Marka - string; brand of vacuum cleaner.
- Wiek kupującego - age of customer.
- Płeć kupującego - gender of customer.
- Ocena - customer rating.

## data_fin.csv
This file contains cumulated information from data.csv without NaN values.
- DniOdZakupu - numerical variable; days since purchase. 
- Marka - string; brand of vacuum cleaner.
- Wiek - age of customer.
- Płeć - gender of customer.
- Ocena - customer rating.
